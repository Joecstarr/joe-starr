For the low low cost of 35 dollars you can support the
[Mathematics Genealogy Project](https://www.genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/) by
[purchasing a genealogy poster](https://www.genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/posters.php).
Information about the project can be found in their
[mission statement](https://www.genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/mission.php).

In addition to the printable poster my purchase came with a computer readable version.
Displayed below is an interactive version of
[my](https://www.genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/id.php?id=345388) genealogy.
The interactive layer is built with
[react-sigma](https://www.genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/id.php?id=345388).
